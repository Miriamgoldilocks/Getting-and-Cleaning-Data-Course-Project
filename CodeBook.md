-   activity - type of activity the subject was doing while measurement
    were taken

Activities
==========

-   WALKING :

    subject was walking during measurements

-   WALKING\_UPSTAIRS :

    subject was walking up strairs during measurements

-   WALKING\_DOWNSTAIRS :

    subject was walking down strairs during measurements

-   SITTING :

    subject was sitting during measurements

-   STANDING :

    subject was standing during measurements

-   LAYING :

    subject was laying during measurements
